editTips and Tricks

Below are 10 tips and tricks on how to write terrifying horror.

  1. Use strong tone and layer it with darkness.

  2. Give antagonists/villians realistic motivation - make them credible.

  3. Incorporate elements of tragedy (e.g., cruelty, danger).

  4. Base the horror on natural/actual human fears.

  5. Note that horror incorporates terror (the feeling of deep fear) but also adds a layer of revulsion and surprise.

  6. Look at horror with a fresh perspective and try new things.

  7. Identify how the events of your story will happen chronologically and how they will happen narratively.

  8. Keep the suspense going.

  9. While it is good to have a plan, don't resist deviating from it - listen to your story and where it wants to go.

  10. The element of the unknown is key.

For more information, visit the following websites from which the above points were gathered:

https://www.nownovel.com/blog/how-to-write-horror-story-tips/

http://www.writersdigest.com/editor-blogs/there-are-no-rules/the-horror-genre-on-writing-horror-and-avoiding-cliches

http://www.openculture.com/2014/10/h-p-lovecraft-gives-five-tips-for-drafting-a-horror-story-or-any-piece-of-weird-fiction.html

editHow to Write Horror

ISBN: 978-0399165559

Publication Date: February 20, 2014

Now Write! Science Fiction, Fantasy, and Horror’s purpose is to provide a toolbox of resources for creating an alternative reality.

This book contains information on how to construct a title for a story, how the richness and variety of words can be used to create terrifying stories, and how to make ficitonal characters feel real and relatable.

Book on Amazon

ISBN: 978-1582974200

Publication Date: November 18, 2006

On Writing Horror’s purpose is to provide a resource to help create terrifyingly dark stories.

This book contains information on how to write primeval violence, the psychology behind traditional horror, mistakes to avoid when writing horror, and how to create and use characters and settings that contribute to the instinctual fear of the unknown.

Book on Amazon

ISBN: 978-1499324891

Publication Date: September 4, 2014

Writing Dark Stories’ purpose is to teach writers how to haunt readers with their characters.

This book contains information on how to create powerfully impactful plots, how to construct chilling characters, how to identify and utilize fear when writing, and how to create a story that will make the readers question their view of the world.

Book on Amazon

ISBN: 978-1599638089

Publication Date: September 18, 2014

Writing Monsters’ purpose is to provide information and tools for writers to craft terrifying monsters.

This book contains information relating to identifying what monsters are, what makes a monster scary, and what is the difference between a monster and a villian; the purpose of the monster in the story; and how to write monsters’ descriptions, actions, and powers/weaknesses.
