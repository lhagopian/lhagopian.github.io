NaNoWriMo

National Novel Writing Month (NaNoWriMo) is a self-assigned writing competition that takes place in the month of November.  Writers from all over the world partake in the goal of writing 50,000 words in the month of November – starting from the 1st and ending on the 30th. 

  • A writer should not begin writing their NaNoWriMo story until November 1st – they are, however, able to plan it out before.
  • The daily goal is to write 1,667 words each day to reach the end goal. 
  • Stories that have been written during the NaNoWriMo challenge have been published.
  • Because of the wide geographic range of participants, there are groups established in different communities for writers to come together during November to support and encourage each other to reach the goal.
  • The hosts of NaNoWriMo provide tips and tricks leading up to November, as well as words of encouragement during the month of November.

NaNoWriMo, in addition to being a fun challenge to try to accomplish, also works to create a deep sense of community with people all around the world, as everyone works towards the same goal.

https://nanowrimo.org/

Wattpad

Wattpad is an online community for writers.  The purpose of Wattpad is threefold: to provide a place to share stories, to provide a place to read others' stories, and to create a community that helps writers grow as storytellers through feedback and encouragement.  Wattpad offers a safe place for people to:

  • Read stories from a wide array of genres and provide feedback if they choose;
  • Post stories from various genres and gain feedback from fellow writers or avid readers;
  • Partake in a number of genre-related contests;
  • Gain resources and skills that could lead to having their story published. 

In addition to providing a safe space for writers to share their work online, Wattpad helps to build a sense of community where people can feel encouraged to share their writing.

http://wattpad.com/home

editWhere to Publish Your Story


ISBN: 978-1440352669

Publication Date: September 14, 2017

Guide to Literary Agents 2018’s purpose is to provide a concise, current guide to literary agents, letting authors know to whom to go to publish.

This book contains information relating to the benefits of having a literary agent, a listing and description of the many literary agents, agents divided by genre, a year subscription to WritersMarket.com literary agents content, and access to a webinar relating to finding a literary agent.

Book on Amazon

ISBN: 978-1440352638

Publication Date: August 31, 2017

Writer’s Market 2018’s purpose is to provide a concise, current guide to the market for writers.

This book contains information relating to the various ways to publish one's work (e.g., publishing companies, magazines, contests), what information is needed to submit work to the various locations, how to promote written work, types of writing organizations, sample query letters, and access to a webinar relating to funding a writing career.

Book on Amazon

ISBN: 978-1912105854

Publication Date: August 3, 2017

Successful Self-Publishing’s purpose is to provide a comprehensive guide to self-publishing.

This book describes the information one should know before self-publishing, the benefits of self-publishing an ebook, how to publish an ebook, the benefits of self-publishing a print book, how to publish a print book, the steps that should be taken after publication, the cost of self-publication, how to gain a profit, and how to market a self-published book.
